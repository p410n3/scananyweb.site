About

This WebService was made after testing a bunch of WebServers of some clients for obvious security problems like open VNC ports, outdated and vulnerable wordpress versions and similiar stuff. The thought "having these simple (yet powerful) tools on a website, available anywhere and not only my laptop or own vServer would be nice man" crossed my mind, so I decided to just do it.

I hope you are statisfied with the Service but keep in mind:

ONLY SCAN STUFF YOU ARE EXPRESSLY ALLOWED TO SCAN!
